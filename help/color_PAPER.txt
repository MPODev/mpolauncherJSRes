color_PAPER.jsx

----------

This script sets color of the selection to PAPER [C= 0, M = 0, Y = 0, K = 0]
It will change the colors of:
- images (bitmaps)
- polygons (vectorforms)
- rectangles (basic boxes)
- paragraphs (all text in the selected paraphs)
- rulers above text
- rulers below text

----------

PREREQUISITES:

1. Just a selection

----------

USAGE:

Select an object on your page and hit "RUN".
